A Legacy of Technical Excellence

Smt Tamma Kotamma Reddy Polytechnic is a dream realized by an inspired group of eminent intellectuals, established to impart quality Technical Education to youth in rural areas. Sponsored bsindu and High Branch School Society, Machilipatnam — a society with roots datingkhe polytechnic was founded in 1987-88.

Affiliated to the State Board of Technical Education & Training, Andhra Pradesh and approved by AICTE, New Delhi, the polytechnic is located in Pamarru ohrom Vijayawada to Machilipatnam in Krishna District.

Campus Life

Life at TKR Polytechnic

State-of-the-art facilities, comfortable hostels, a well-stocked library, and vibrant campus culture — everything for a holistic educational experience.

Boys' Hostel

A well-maintained hostel with 80 rooms, accommodating up to 400 students. Facilities include a TV room, indoor games rooms, and a mineral water plant. The hostel daily provides breakfast (with milk/coffee), lunch, and dinner.

80 Rooms 400 Capacity ₹4,500/Year

Girls' Hostel

A secure hostel with 15 rooms and 2 common halls, accommodating 125 students. Features include a TV room, mineral water plant, and strict discipline. A dedicated Hostel Committee ensures a safe and comfortable environment.

15 Rooms 125 Capacity ₹4,500/Year

Library & Resources

A comprehensive library housing an extensive collection of textbooks, reference journals, reference materials, and previous year question papers. The library serves as a vital resource center for academic studies and exam preparation.
